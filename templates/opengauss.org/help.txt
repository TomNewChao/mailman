$listname邮件列表的帮助
Help for $listname mailing list

这是$domain域的版本为$version的电子邮件命令“帮助”。以下描述了您可以发送以获取的命令,关于您在本网站订阅
Mailman列表的信息和管理。命令可以位于主题行或邮件正文中。
This is email command 'help' for version $version of the Mailman list manager at $domain.
The following describes commands you can send to get information about and control your
subscription to Mailman lists at this site. A command can be in the Subject line or in
the body of the message.

命令应发送到 ${listname}-request@${domain} 地址。
Commands should be sent to the ${listname}-request@${domain} address.

以下命令有效：
The following commands are valid:

    $commands

需要引起人们注意的问题和疑虑应发送至：
Questions and concerns for the attention of a person should be sent to:

    $administrator
